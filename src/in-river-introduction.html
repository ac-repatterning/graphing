RIVER LEVELS

The river levels hub conducts river level series anomaly detection, provides river levels forecasts during weather warning periods, and provides supplementary metrics, e.g., weighted rates of change of river levels, daily quantiles, etc. The hub is predominantly for officials aiding Scottish Government Resilience activities.

Future possibilities include forecasts activated & weighted-rate-of-change activated alerts. For more information, including code, setup and configurations, etc. visit GitHub
